Technology use in Spain as a sustainable approach to public transportation

Using smart design and innovative uses of technology, Albacete, Spain has made dramatic improvements to the public transit system. Through the use of technology, preferential treatment at traffic lights, real time information, and a smartphone app with a CO2 calculator are increasing ridership have been implemented and are earning the system recognition for the benefits it offers to health, safety, and the environment.
