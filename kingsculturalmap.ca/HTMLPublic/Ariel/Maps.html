Maps by Ariel Vallis

Hiking trails map for Fundy National Park in New Brunswick, Canada. Designed in CorelDraw software. The inspiration for the design came from the bright colours in vintage national park/tourism posters and maps. View here.

Soils erodibility map for the town of Digby, Nova Scotia. Constructed for the GIS course using slope and soil texture, calculated and placed on top of a basemap. Constructed using ArcGIS software. View here.

Canada Land Inventory forestry data for the Annapolis-Kings study area. This map was part of a biophysical atlas, combining environmental planning, GIS and cartographic design. The map shows the land capability for forestry growth. Constructed using ArcGIS software. View here.

Basemap for the Town of Digby, Nova Scotia. Served as a foundation GIS project to learn how to construct a basemap. Also served as a base for many thematic maps, such as the soils erodibility map above. This was a semester long project and will serve as a portfolio piece for a long time. Constructed using ArcGIS software. View here.
